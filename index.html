Sports

Sports and games also boost self-esteem and confidence, which may make it easier for kids to deal with tough situations at home or at school. One way to encourage students to engage in sports and games is by offering them access to facilities where they can participate. We provide all the equipment and facilities to students to encourage the participation of students in sports and games.

Outdoor Games

Availability of well-maintained fields and open gym along with a wide range of training equipment for games like Football, Boxing, Volleyball, Basketball, Handball, etc. to inculcate sportsmanship in our cadets, promising them ample opportunities in state and national level sports tournaments under the guidance of skilled and experienced coaches.

Indoor Games

Where strategy meets agility, Our indoor sports arena inspires teamwork and skill. From chessboards to badminton courts, Cadets compete with passion and precision. Nurturing sharp minds and disciplined bodies, They develop qualities of true sportsmanship.
