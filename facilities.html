Our Facilities

With best Education our Schools also provides best kind of Facilities.

E-Learning

We intend to embrace our cadets with highly specialized Interactive digital panels and 3D Virtual reality room. Various e-learning facilities provided by us like recorded lectures, animated explanations, a wide range of online study materials, quizzes, and e-books help breed better learning experiences and clearer illustration of concepts along with their applications to the cadets along with their exceptional academic growth.

Hostel

Hostel rooms are an integral part of our institute. We believe in building a fresh and stress free environment for our cadets with highly ventilated and limitedly accommodated rooms where cadets will be supervised by rectors.

Cadet's Mess/Canteen

We ensure nutritional meals to our cadets thrice a day along with evening snacks and milk that is yielded from our own reputed ‘ JAI BHADRA DAIRY FARM ’. Feedback from cadets and parents will be actively sought to ensure that the mess facility evolves to meet the changing expectations.

Transportation

Our school recognizes the need to bridge the gap between home. Having a fleet of buses is an effective way to provide transportation to day scholars who live within a certain radius of the school. We promise your child the highest standards of a safe journey along with trained drivers and transportation staff.

Science Laboratory

A space for exploration and discovery, The science lab ignites young minds. Cadets experiment, question, and innovate, Turning curiosity into groundbreaking ideas. Here, they learn to unravel the mysteries of the world, Equipped with the tools of knowledge.

Military Training

Our school's commitment to the physical development of cadets is evident in the provision of military training facilities under the supervision of defence retired officers.

Pottery

Crafting creativity with their own hands, Cadets shape raw clay into beautiful forms. Every pot tells a story of patience and skill, Reflecting artistry and imagination. Pottery teaches them focus and dedication, Turning simplicity into timeless beauty.

Dance Department

Expressing stories through movement, Cadets combine discipline with creativity. From classical to modern styles, They perform with grace and energy. Every dance step reflects their passion, Showcasing tradition and innovation together.

Music Department

Harmonizing tradition with talent, The music department nurtures creativity. Cadets learn to play, sing, and compose, Creating melodies that inspire and uplift. Every note they play reflects dedication, Celebrating the universal language of music.

Obstacle

An obstacle is an object, thing, action or situation that causes an obstruction. Different types of obstacles include physical, economic, biopsychosocial, cultural, political, technological and military.The obstacle course is composed of seven major obstacles separated by six low hurdles. The course begins by vaulting over a low hurdle and then taking on the single high bar. On this event two different techniques are demonstrated, college boy roll or a kip. Participants may use either of these techniques.

Horse Riding

We wish to build fearless cadets to lead the nation, and hence we put forth a touch of elegance, excitement, and a connection between humans and animals with the horse-riding facility. Cadets will be provided training by skillful professionals, contributing to their overall cardiovascular growth.

Swimming

A facility provided by us that sets us apart is SWIMMING. We promise to provide a well-maintained pool along with all the swimming facilities and coaches to engage our cadets in regular muscular activities along with strengthening their lungs and heart with improved flexibility. Join us to stay active and make a splash!

Medical Facility

Along with providing a highly nutritious meal, the establishment of healthcare services within our school is a transformative step that places the well-being of our cadets at the forefront of our priorities. Our healthcare facility is staffed with qualified and trained medical professionals, including doctors and nurses who will be doing timely checkups of the cadets and provide required treatment.

24/7 Security

The provision of security to our cadets on campus is the top priority of the institute. We understand the concerns of parents and hence we are committed to establishing highly effective security measures for our cadets. Cadets are under CCTV surveillance for 24/7 hours and are taken care of by wardens who are all army retired professionals, along with carefully monitored and controlled access points to the campus.

Hostel

Hostel rooms are an integral part of our institute. We believe in building a fresh and stress free environment for our cadets with highly ventilated and limitedly accommodated rooms where cadets will be supervised by rectors.

Cadet's Mess/Canteen

Our canteen facility is not just about providing meals; we provide organic food for our cadets. Our mess has a modern kitchen maintained effectively under hygienic conditions. We ensure nutritional meals to our cadets thrice a day along with evening snacks and milk that is yielded from our own reputed ‘JAI BHADRA DAIRY FARM’. Feedback from cadets and parents will be actively sought to ensure that the mess facility evolves to meet the changing expectations.

Laundry

Providing a laundry facility in our school will alleviate a significant burden on our Cadets. We consider factors such as space allocation, equipment procurement, staffing, and maintenance of school uniforms and sports garments thus improving the attire of our cadets.

Haircut

As our cadets are the real defence aspirants, it is our duty to inculcate officer like qualities and appearance in them. haircut facility at our school would promote a sense of pride and confidence among our cadets. Monthly haircuts would be provided to the cadets by professional hairdressers.
